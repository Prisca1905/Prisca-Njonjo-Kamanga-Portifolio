About

I'm a recent BSc Agricultural Economics graduate with 2 years of hands-on experience in monitoring and evaluation, survey design, quantitative research, and policy analysis. I work at the intersection of agricultural development, green economy, and data-driven decision-making—helping development organisations and agribusinesses measure impact, understand markets, and inform strategy through rigorous research.

Currently, I support the AFR100 ESO Programme and DOT Going Beyond at mHub Malawi, while researching green economy skills for the African School of Green Economy. I'm also the founder of Tiwale Agro-Enterprise, a digital network connecting smallholder farmers with markets and finance.
